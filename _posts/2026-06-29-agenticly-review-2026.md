## What Is Agenticly?

[Agenticly](https://agenticly.app) is a mobile-first AI analytics app for retail investors, available on iOS and Android. The core idea is simple: you deploy autonomous AI agents that analyze real-time market data across both cryptocurrency and US equities, then deliver personalized insights back to you.

Agents cover on-chain data for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana alongside US stock market movements — all in a single app. On-chain data is activity recorded directly on a blockchain: wallet flows, transaction volumes, network activity, and other signals that reflect what's actually happening with an asset, not just what its price is doing.

You can schedule agents to run on a recurring basis, so your research happens automatically without you pulling data or logging in to check anything. Wake up to insights. That's the practical value proposition.

The app is freemium. There's a free tier you can access without a credit card, and paid tiers exist for more advanced use — though specific pricing isn't publicly listed.

## Core Features

### Scheduled AI Agent Runs

This is what separates Agenticly from every passive dashboard on the market. Set an agent to run on a schedule — daily, for example — and it delivers research at the cadence you choose. No manual trigger. No logging in to check. The market never sleeps. Neither do your agents.

### On-Chain Data Decoding for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana

Most retail investors don't have the time or background to interpret raw on-chain data themselves. Agenticly decodes it and surfaces what's relevant to your portfolio or interests. For Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana, that means signals beyond price — network health, flow patterns, activity trends. The kind of data institutional desks pay serious money to access.

### Cross-Asset Coverage: Crypto and US Equities Together

This is genuinely rare. If you hold both crypto and stocks — and most self-directed retail investors in 2026 do — you've probably been stitching together multiple apps just to get a complete picture. Agenticly covers both in one place, with agents that track US equity movements alongside on-chain crypto data at the same time.

### Personalized Insights

Agents don't deliver generic market summaries. The insights are tied to your portfolio and interests. If you care about Solana and a handful of tech stocks, your agents focus there — not on the entire market.

## How It Compares to the Competition in 2026

The retail research tool landscape has shifted noticeably this year. Here's where the main alternatives stand.

**Glassnode** remains the most data-rich on-chain platform for Bitcoin and Ethereum, with over 1,500 metrics and years of historical data. But it's crypto-only, has no agent scheduling, and requires you to interpret everything manually. Its Advanced plan runs $49 per month on an annual basis. Deep data, no automation — and it won't touch your stock portfolio.

**Nansen** is strong for Ethereum and Solana wallet labeling and smart money tracking. Pioneer plan runs roughly $120 per year; Professional is $1,200 per year. No equities coverage, no agent automation. Its Trustpilot average sits at 2.9 out of 5 as of 2026.

**Token Metrics** covers AI-generated ratings across 6,000-plus crypto projects and has integrated on-chain trade execution. Starting around $49 per month, it's crypto-only and built more around execution than scheduled research delivery.

**Messari** is worth flagging specifically because of what happened in June 2026: following its acquisition by Blockworks, Messari retired its Lite and Pro retail plans. If you were relying on it for retail-grade research, that option is gone. The platform has moved firmly toward enterprise clients.

**Kaito AI** aggregates Web3 social signals and is useful for tracking narrative momentum, but it isn't a structured investment research tool. No on-chain data depth, no equities, no agent scheduling.

The gap Agenticly fills is specific: no established competitor has combined scheduled AI agents, on-chain crypto data for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana, and US equities coverage in a single consumer-facing mobile app. That's not a marketing claim — it's a structural observation about what each platform actually does.

## Who Should Use Agenticly

Agenticly is built for self-directed retail investors who already manage their own portfolios and want to cut the time they spend on manual research — without giving up control over their decisions.

You'll get the most out of it if you hold a mix of crypto (particularly Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Solana) and US stocks, check markets daily but don't have hours to spend on research, want signals tied to your actual portfolio rather than generic alerts, are comfortable with mobile-first tools, and want on-chain data context without becoming a blockchain analyst yourself.

If you're a pure equities investor with no crypto exposure, the on-chain features won't apply to you. If you're an institutional trader or quant who needs API access and raw data exports, this isn't built for that use case.
