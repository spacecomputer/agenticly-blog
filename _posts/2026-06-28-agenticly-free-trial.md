You found Agenticly, you want to try it, and you have one question: what exactly do you get for free?

That's fair. "Free tier" can mean anything from a fully functional product to a locked-down preview that cuts you off after five minutes. This article breaks down exactly what the Agenticly free tier includes, who it's built for, and whether it's worth your time before you think about upgrading.

## What Is the Agenticly Free Tier?

Agenticly is a mobile-first AI analytics app for retail investors managing both crypto and US stock portfolios. The core concept is agents — autonomous AI agents you deploy to research markets, decode on-chain data, and deliver personalized insights directly to your phone.

The free tier gets you into that agent-based research experience without a credit card. Sign up, deploy agents, start receiving insights. That's the entry point.

You can get started at [Agenticly](https://agenticly.app/) from the website, or download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play.

## What the Free Tier Actually Includes

### AI Agent Deployment

Agents are the product. And the free tier lets you deploy them. Your agents analyze real-time market data across Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and US equities. You're not browsing a static dashboard — you're putting agents to work on your behalf.

On-chain data is the raw activity recorded directly on a blockchain: wallet movements, transaction volumes, network activity. Most retail investors never touch this data because making sense of it takes time and expertise they don't have. Agenticly's agents decode it and surface what's relevant to your portfolio, no blockchain background required.

### Crypto and US Equities in One Place

This is one of the more meaningful things the free tier unlocks. Most analytics platforms make you choose: crypto or stocks. Agenticly covers both in a single interface.

Holding Bitcoin alongside tech stocks? Watching Solana while tracking earnings season? Your agents can research both at the same time. No second app. No second subscription.

### Scheduled Agent Runs

You can schedule agents to run on a recurring basis. Set the schedule, and research arrives automatically. You wake up to insights instead of spending your morning pulling data from five different sources.

This is what separates Agenticly from a passive analytics dashboard. A dashboard shows you data when you open it. A scheduled agent works while you're asleep, in a meeting, or simply not thinking about markets.

### Personalized Insights

Your agents don't send generic market summaries. The insights are tied to your portfolio and interests. If you're focused on Ethereum and a handful of US equities, your agents research those specifically — not a broadcast feed built for every investor on the platform.

## How the Free Tier Compares to Paying for Alternatives

Some context is useful here. The retail analytics market in 2026 has a real gap.

Messari retired its Lite and Pro retail plans in June 2026 following its Blockworks acquisition, effectively stepping away from individual investors. Glassnode's Advanced plan costs $49 per month, covers Bitcoin and Ethereum only, has no agent automation, and no equities. Nansen's Pioneer plan runs roughly $120 per year, focuses on Ethereum and Solana wallet tracking, and has neither equities coverage nor any scheduling layer. Token Metrics starts at around $49 per month and is crypto-only.

None of these platforms offer scheduled AI agents. None combine on-chain crypto data with US equities in a single retail-facing app.

Agenticly's free tier gives you access to the only platform doing all three: on-chain coverage for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana; US equities tracking; and scheduled autonomous agents. You can evaluate all of that before spending a dollar.

## Who Gets the Most Out of the Free Tier

The free tier fits a specific kind of investor well. If you actively manage a portfolio that spans both crypto and US stocks, already use tools like Robinhood, Coinbase, or TradingView but feel buried in data, want research delivered to you rather than having to hunt for it, and are comfortable with technology even if you've never read raw blockchain data — you'll get a genuine, functional experience here. You're not being handed a demo. You're deploying agents and receiving real insights.

If you're a pure crypto trader who wants 1,500 on-chain metrics to interpret manually, a specialized platform like Glassnode might suit your workflow better. Agenticly is built for investors who want clarity, not raw data dumps.

## What the Free Tier Doesn't Tell You

Agenticly doesn't publicly list the differences between its free and paid tiers. What's clear is that the free entry point is designed to give you meaningful access to the core agent experience. If you hit a ceiling and want more agent runs, higher scheduling frequency, or additional features, paid tiers are available.

The honest framing: start free, see what your agents surface, and decide from there. You're not locked in, and you're not being asked to pay before you've seen the product work.
