## What Each Platform Does

### Agenticly

[Agenticly](https://agenticly.app/) is a mobile-first AI analytics app built for retail investors who hold both crypto and US stocks. The core concept is agents: autonomous AI agents you deploy to research Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and US equity markets on your behalf.

You schedule an agent, it runs automatically, and you wake up to personalized insights based on your portfolio and interests. On-chain data sits at the heart of the crypto coverage. On-chain data means activity recorded directly on a blockchain — wallet flows, transaction volumes, network usage — signals that often move ahead of price and are notoriously difficult to interpret manually. Agenticly decodes that data and surfaces what matters to you, without requiring you to become a blockchain analyst.

The app covers Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana for on-chain analysis and tracks US stock market movements in the same interface. Available on iOS and Android, with a free tier that requires no credit card.

### Token Metrics

Token Metrics is a crypto-focused research platform offering AI-generated ratings and scores across more than 6,000 crypto projects. It provides price predictions, technical analysis, and sentiment signals, and integrates on-chain trade execution through a partnership with LiFi. Plans start at approximately $49 per month.

The platform is built around helping traders identify which crypto projects to buy or avoid. Its AI scoring system generates grades for individual tokens, and the overall focus leans toward execution — helping investors act on signals rather than just understand them.

## Where Token Metrics Has the Edge

Token Metrics covers a wider universe of crypto projects. If you are actively scanning altcoins, want AI-generated scores on smaller tokens, or need a platform that connects directly to trade execution, it has built real infrastructure for that.

The ratings system is useful when you want a quick read on a token's fundamentals without digging through whitepapers. For traders who are primarily crypto-focused and want to move fast on signals, that execution layer has genuine appeal.

---

## Where Agenticly Has the Edge

The gap becomes clear when you look at what Token Metrics cannot do.

**No scheduled agent automation.** Token Metrics delivers ratings and signals, but you still have to log in, check the dashboard, and interpret what you see. Agenticly's agents run on a schedule you set. Research arrives without you having to pull it.

**No US equities coverage.** Most retail investors in 2026 hold both crypto and stocks. Token Metrics is crypto-only. Managing a mixed portfolio across Ethereum and tech stocks means running two separate platforms. With Agenticly, one agent handles both.

**No on-chain depth for retail.** Token Metrics includes some on-chain signals, but the platform's focus is ratings and execution, not deep on-chain decoding. Agenticly is built around on-chain data for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana as the primary signal layer — translating blockchain activity into plain insights you can act on.

**No free entry point.** Token Metrics requires payment from day one. Agenticly's free tier lets you test the agents and see how they work before committing to anything.

## The Automation Gap Is the Real Difference

Token Metrics is a research dashboard. You open it, review ratings, and decide what to do. That model still demands your time and attention every day.

Agenticly is built around a different premise: your agents do the research while you are not watching. You define what matters, schedule the runs, and receive insights tailored to your portfolio. The market never sleeps. Neither do your agents.

For investors who check markets daily but are drowning in signals, that automation gap matters more than the number of tokens rated. A score on 6,000 projects does not help much if you do not have time to review them.
