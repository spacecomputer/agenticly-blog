AI market research agents aren't just for hedge funds anymore. Agenticly has launched a mobile app that puts autonomous, schedulable AI agents directly in retail investors' hands — covering Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and US equities in one place, with zero coding required.

The app is live now on iOS and Android across more than 150 countries.

## What This Means for Retail Investors

The timing matters. In June 2026, Messari retired its retail-facing Lite and Pro plans after its Blockworks acquisition, pulling one of the few independent crypto research platforms built for self-directed investors off the table. At the same time, brokerages are moving fast: Public.com has launched AI agents tied to its own platform, and Charles Schwab has introduced AI-powered portfolio insights — but both live inside their respective ecosystems, which means your research is shaped by where you happen to hold your account.

Agenticly takes a different approach. It's brokerage-agnostic, mobile-first, and built for retail investors who want research intelligence without being locked into any single financial institution.

The broader market signals strong demand for exactly this. The global AI agents market is valued at $10.9 billion in 2026 and projected to reach $182.9 billion by 2033, growing at a 49.6% CAGR according to Grand View Research. Retail investors are entering this shift at the right moment — provided they have the right tools.

## Four Agent Types, One Mobile App

Agenticly is built around a single core concept: agents. Not dashboards. Not static alerts. Agents you deploy, schedule, and direct toward the questions that actually matter to your portfolio.

The app ships with four distinct types:

**Market Tracking Agents** monitor real-time price movements, volume shifts, and on-chain activity across Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana alongside US equity markets. Instead of refreshing five different apps, one agent runs continuous surveillance on the assets you care about.

**Research Agents** go deeper. They decode on-chain data — the raw transactional and network activity recorded on a blockchain — and translate it into plain-language insights. You get the signal without needing to interpret wallet flows or miner behavior yourself.

**Trade Preparation Agents** synthesize market conditions, recent price action, and relevant data points so you can enter a position with more context. These agents don't tell you what to trade. They make sure you're not walking into a decision blind.

**Strategy Evaluation Agents** assess how a current or hypothetical position holds up against live market conditions. Already in a trade or considering one? This agent stress-tests the logic behind it.

All four types can be scheduled to run automatically. You set the cadence. The agents work while you're not watching.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What are AI market research agents and how do they work for retail investors?**

AI market research agents are autonomous programs you deploy to analyze market data on your behalf. Agenticly's agents pull real-time on-chain data from Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana alongside US equity market data, process it, and deliver personalized insights — without you manually reviewing raw data feeds. Schedule them to run on a recurring basis and research arrives automatically.

**Do I need coding or technical skills to use Agenticly?**

No. The app is built for retail investors who aren't developers or on-chain data experts. You configure your agents through the mobile interface, select the assets or strategies you want covered, set a schedule, and you're done. No code required at any point.

**What's the difference between the four agent types?**

Market Tracking Agents monitor live price and on-chain activity. Research Agents decode on-chain data into readable insights. Trade Preparation Agents compile relevant context before you enter a position. Strategy Evaluation Agents assess how a current or planned trade holds up against market conditions. Each type serves a different stage of your research and decision process.

**How is Agenticly different from a brokerage's built-in AI tools?**

Brokerage AI tools are embedded within a single platform and may reflect that platform's product interests. Agenticly is brokerage-agnostic — it doesn't custody assets or execute trades. It works alongside whatever brokerage or exchange you already use, delivering independent research with no conflict of interest tied to your order flow.

**What does pricing include and is there a free option?**

Agenticly offers a 7-day free trial with full platform access. Paid plans are $19.99 per month or $99.99 per year, with 6,000 query credits included. A free tier is also available for investors who want to explore the platform before committing.

**Which assets does Agenticly cover?**

The platform covers Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana through on-chain data decoding, and US equities through real-time stock market tracking. Both asset classes are available within the same mobile app and can be monitored by the same agents simultaneously.

**Is Agenticly available outside the United States?**

Yes. The app launched across more than 150 countries and is available on both the Apple App Store and Google Play. Investors worldwide can download it and start a free trial immediately.
